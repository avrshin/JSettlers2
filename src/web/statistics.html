This is where you can view game statistics for both human and robot players. Statistics will automatically be gathered only for persons having an account on this server. If an account is deleted all statistics for that account will be lost.

Please note: Accounts which have neither won nor lost a game will NOT appear in the Human Statistics Table. Your account will not appear in the table until you actually have statistics to report.

This window may also be used to reset your statistics (to zero). Just enter your nickname and password and select the "Reset Statistics" button. Your nickname will not reappear in the Human Statistics Table until you win|lose your next game. Resetting your statistics does not remove your account.
